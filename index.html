Our Rich History

Founded in 1892

Liverpool FC was formed in 1892 and has grown to become one of the most successful football clubs in England and Europe.

Anfield, our historic home ground with its famous Kop stand, has been our fortress since the club's formation.

Club Legends

Steven Gerrard

Steven Gerrard, our former captain and local hero, is considered one of Liverpool's greatest ever players. His leadership in the 2005 Champions League final, known as the "Miracle of Istanbul," cemented his legendary status.

Gerrard made 710 appearances for Liverpool between 1998-2015, scoring 186 goals from midfield.

Current Stars

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah, known as the "Egyptian King," has been a revelation since joining Liverpool in 2017. His incredible goalscoring record and humble personality have made him a fan favorite.

Salah has won multiple Golden Boot awards and helped Liverpool win the Premier League in 2020 and the Champions League in 2019.
